GROW Your Story

Welcome! I was created by Baked Roots, the South African non-profit organisation. Local community members, like you, grow apple trees, and the apples are donated to bakeries we have partnered with. These bakeries then use the apples to make baked goods for schools, shelters, and those in need—all while promoting sustainability.

You will care for a digital apple tree, and while you do, you will learn the best farming practices and make decisions that affect your tree, fruit, soil, and the health of those who ‘eat’ your apples.

[User], you stand in the middle of the aisle of a busy Builders Warehouse, glancing down at your crumpled shopping list that you hastily wrote this morning. However, you can barely decipher the writing, so you check your basket: garden gloves, check; shears, check; hat, check; soil… Oh, how could you forget the most important item? You weave through the trolleys and make your way to the garden section, only to be met with what feels like an endless selection of different soils. You know how crucial this decision is for the future of your apple tree. Which soil will you choose?

Loamy Soil

✅ Advantages: Ideal for most plants, nutrient and mineral rich, and good water retention.

❌ Disadvantage: Prone to erosion, may need fertiliser to maintain fertility.

Clay Soil

✅ Advantages: Low erosion risk, good water retention, and supports roots.

❌ Disadvantage: Hard to work with, can cause waterlogging, root rot and prevent root growth.

Organic Fertiliser

✅ Advantages: Safe for individuals, eco-friendly and boots plant growth.

❌ Disadvantage: Potential pathogens, slow nutrient release and cost and availability.

Chemical Fertiliser

✅ Advantages: Readily available and affordable.

❌ Disadvantage: Water pollution, soil degradation, can kill beneficial soil microbes.

Flood Irrigation

✅ Advantages: Low cost, no pump needed, and easy to use.

❌ Disadvantage: High water usage, soil erosion, and potential for waterlogging.

Drip Irrigation

✅ Advantages: Lower water usage, even water distribution, and improved plant health.

❌ Disadvantage: Higher costs, could clog, and harder set-up and maintenance.
